About Me

I am a National Institute of Design student studying Master of Design in Film and Video Communication Design. I have a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering and experience in different fields.

My career goal is to become a T-Shaped Designer with a breadth of knowledge in User experience design. I'm currently working as an Associate Product Designer at Tekion Crop.

My hobby is playing fencing, which made me travel across India to win a few national medals. My education and my hobby gave me a chance to meet many people, which taught me the importance of empathy and attention to detail, which I've combined with my user experience skills to understand a user's problems. That makes me more interested in User-Centric design and UX research.
